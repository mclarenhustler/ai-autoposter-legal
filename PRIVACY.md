# Privacy Policy for AI Autoposter

This application ("AI Autoposter") is a private automation tool created by the developer to upload AI-generated videos to TikTok using TikTok’s official API.

## Data Collection
- No personal data from other users is collected, stored, or shared.
- No cookies, analytics, or tracking tools are used.
- The app operates only on the developer’s TikTok accounts.

## Authorization
- All TikTok interactions are authorized through TikTok OAuth.
- The app only uploads videos to the developer’s own channels.
